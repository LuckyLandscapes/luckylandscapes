Garden Bed
Questions Answered

When is the best time to install garden beds in Lincoln, NE?

Spring (April–May) and fall (September–October) are ideal for garden bed installation in Lincoln. Spring gives plants the whole growing season to establish roots, while fall planting benefits from cooler temps and less watering stress. We can install beds year-round as conditions allow.

How deep should mulch be in garden beds?

We recommend 2–3 inches of mulch for most garden beds. This suppresses weeds, retains moisture, and regulates soil temperature. For brand new beds, we may go slightly deeper. Avoid piling mulch against plant stems or tree trunks — it can cause rot.

Can you transplant existing plants to new locations?

Absolutely! We regularly transplant shrubs, perennials, and small trees. Success depends on timing, root ball size, and new site conditions. We'll assess each plant and advise on the best approach. Request a quote to discuss your transplanting needs.
