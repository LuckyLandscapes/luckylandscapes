About this area

Local Crew, Local Knowledge

Waverly is a great little town to work in — quieter, more space, and a different feel than Lincoln proper. We service residential properties throughout the 68462 zip code, including out toward Davey and the rural acreages off Bluff Road.

FAQ

Common Questions From This Area

Why is there a $500 minimum for Waverly?

It's a 15-minute drive from our Lincoln base. The minimum makes the trip worthwhile and lets us keep our hourly pricing the same as in-town. Most real landscape projects are well above that anyway.

Do you mow lawns in Waverly?

For larger acreages or as part of a multi-service contract, yes. We don't do single small-lot weekly mows in Waverly because the drive doesn't pencil out — but if you're bundling with cleanup or hardscape, we can include it.

How quickly can you start a project in Waverly?

Most cleanup and lawn jobs we can be on-site within the same week. Hardscape and design projects typically start 1-3 weeks out depending on the season and scope.
