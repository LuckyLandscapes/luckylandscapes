FAQ

Cleanup
Questions Answered

What items do you haul away?

We handle yard waste, brush, furniture, appliances, construction debris, and general junk. Basically if it fits in our trucks, we'll take it. The only things we can't take are hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, etc.) and certain electronics. Call us if you're unsure!

How quickly can you schedule a cleanup?

Most cleanup jobs can be scheduled within the same week. For urgent needs, we often accommodate next-day service. Get a free quote and let us know your timeline.

Do you offer recurring cleanup services?

Yes! We offer one-time cleanups as well as recurring services for properties that need regular maintenance — rental properties, commercial lots, or simply keeping up with seasonal debris. We'll customize a schedule that works for you.
