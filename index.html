What landscaping services does Lucky Landscapes offer in Lincoln, NE?

Lucky Landscapes offers a full range of landscaping services in Lincoln, Nebraska and surrounding areas, including:

  • Lawn Care — Weekly mowing, seasonal cleanups, leaf removal, and year-round maintenance packages
  • Garden Beds & Mulch — New bed installation, mulching, edging, and plant transplanting
  • Hardscaping — Paver patios, walkways, retaining walls, and outdoor living structures
  • Property Cleanup — Junk removal, debris hauling, and overgrown yard restoration
  • Full Landscape Design & Build — Complete outdoor transformations from concept to completion
Does Lucky Landscapes provide free estimates?

Yes! We provide 100% free, no-obligation estimates for all of our services. You can request a quote online in about 2 minutes, or call us directly at (402) 405-5475. We typically respond within 24 hours.

What areas does Lucky Landscapes serve?

We primarily serve Lincoln, Nebraska and the surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm service availability for your specific location.

How quickly can Lucky Landscapes start my project?

Most cleanup and lawn care jobs can be scheduled within the same week. For larger projects like hardscaping and landscape design, we typically begin within 1–3 weeks depending on the scope and season. Get a free quote to discuss your timeline.

Is Lucky Landscapes insured?

Yes, Lucky Landscapes is fully insured to protect both our team and your property during every project. Your peace of mind is our priority.
