Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Lucky Landscapes collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Introduction

Lucky Landscapes ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website luckylandscapes.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our contact form.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us through our website's contact form. This may include:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Service you are interested in
  • Project details or messages you include

We do not collect information automatically through cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools installed on our website.

How We Use Your Information

The information you submit through our contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or quote request
  • Follow up about your landscaping project
  • Communicate with you about our services

When you submit our contact form, your information is sent to a private Google Sheet and forwarded to our email at rileykopf@luckylandscapes.com so that our team can review and respond to your request.

Third-Party Services

We do not install third-party analytics or tracking tools on our website. Our website does not use cookies.

We maintain business profiles on social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube. These platforms may collect their own data about users who visit our profiles — please refer to each platform's privacy policy for details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information is only shared with Google (via Google Sheets) for the purpose of storing and organizing your contact form submission.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information you provide. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for us to use your information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
